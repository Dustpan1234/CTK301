For this project, I had to take one song and seperate it into three ten second sections. The goal was to EQ each section to make it sound different. The words I used to describe each section are thunderous, airy, and mellow. The first section corresponds to the first word, etc.

This one will be shown a future date due to it not loading because of the multitude of files and unsupported format. This piece involved taking a lot of different sounds (with no dialogue) and creating a story with the sounds.
